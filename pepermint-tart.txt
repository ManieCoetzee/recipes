600ml	cream
2 cans	Caramel Condensed milk
3 packets	Malt biscuits

Whip cream untill firm, then add condensed milk and mix well.  Add splash of mint essence and mix well.  mix half of this cream mixture with crushed biscuits and mould into a tart pan.  pour rest of ixture over it and flatten.  Let stand for a day in the fridge. Taste improves with age.
